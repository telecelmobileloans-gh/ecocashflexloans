How It Works

5 Simple Steps to Get Your Bundle

From building your mix to instant activation — the exact journey through EcoCash.

1
🛒

Build & Subscribe

Pick a period, slide Voice, SMS & Data to your mix, then tap Subscribe and review your bundle in the confirmation sheet.

2
✅

Confirm & Pay

Check your Duration, Voice, SMS, Data and total USD amount in the pop-up, then tap Pay with EcoCash to proceed.

3
📱

Enter EcoCash Number & PIN

Enter your ZW +263 number and 4-digit PIN in the secure payment panel to identify your EcoCash wallet.

4
💬

Verify OTP

A one-time PIN is sent to your Econet number by SMS. Enter it on the verification screen to authorise the payment.

5
⚡

Bundle Activated Instantly

Payment confirmed — your bundle is live immediately. Dial *143# on your Econet line anytime to check your remaining balance.
