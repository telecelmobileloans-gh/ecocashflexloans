Got Questions?
Frequently Asked Questions
What currency is used?
▼
All amounts are displayed and processed in ZiG (Zimbabwe Gold), the local currency, throughout the calculator, review, and summary steps.
What is the maximum loan amount?
▼
You can apply for up to ZiG 50,000 with a repayment term of up to 6 months at a flat rate of 10.9% per month.
Is collateral required?
▼
No collateral is required. EcoCash Flex Loans are fully unsecured and based on your EcoCash account history and declared income.
How quickly will I receive the funds?
▼
Once approved — typically within 15 minutes — funds are disbursed directly and instantly to your EcoCash wallet.
How do I repay my loan?
▼
Repayments are made directly through your EcoCash wallet on the agreed monthly schedule. You will receive reminders via your registered mobile number.
