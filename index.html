Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What currency is used on this page?
▼
The page uses ZiG labels throughout the calculator, review step, and final application summary.
Where does the application go after Step 3?
▼
After Step 3, the page saves the application locally and opens login.html with the selected phone, amount, repayment term, fees, total repayment, applicant details, and ZiG currency in the URL.
Is the form connected to an external service?
▼
No. The page does not use external submit calls, chat widgets, Telegram calls, or fetch requests.
